About me

I am largely a self taught junior developer from Sydney, Australia, currently studying the Diploma of Information Technology with Coder Academy. My software environments are .Net and .NetCore but I know my way around Rails and Phoenix aswell. I am excellent within the Linux environment and command line and have been an Archlinux user for many years. I am competent in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, C#, Ruby and Bash. I'm currently furthering my programming knowledge with the functional paradigm via Elixir and Phoenix and have dabbled with Dart and Flutter.

Currently I am Web Master for the Clairvision School of Meditation with whom I am also a Meditation Facilitator.

My previous and current roles all require an ability to lead and work within a team. It is a joy for me to utilise group dynamics in order to achieve the highest desirable outcome for all involved. I am a fast and inquisitive learner with a penchant for finding simple, lateral solutions to seemingly complex problems.
